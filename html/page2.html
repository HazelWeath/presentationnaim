My hobbies

The first hobby I have is playing video games. When I have time, I play video games with my friends. We play all types of games if possible in English and with the nearby chat. This activity allows me to relax during my free time and to develop my English.

My second hobby that I no longer practice at the moment is a sport, it is climbing. Last year, I did climbing outside of class, it was every Wednesday afternoon for two hours after class. This activity allowed me to exert myself while having fun and also allowed me to gain more self-confidence. It is a sport that I will not hesitate to take up again when I have the time and I will have gotten used to the rhythm of studies.

There is also another activity that I have never practiced but that I would like to practice. It is airsoft, It is a sport that has always made me want to, unfortunately in Blois there are not many clubs to do airsoft so I would have to move closer to Tours or Orléans.
